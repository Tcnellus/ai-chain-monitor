## 2026-05-11 Initial Thesis

The AI infrastructure chain remains in expansion. The first-order beneficiaries are well known, especially GPUs and hyperscalers. The current research focus is Step 3: lagging suppliers that could benefit from bottlenecks in power, cooling, grid, advanced packaging, and networking.

Initial candidates:

- `HUBB`: steadier grid/electrical component exposure.
- `AMKR`: advanced packaging and test optionality.
- `PWR`: strong backlog and infrastructure construction exposure.
- `NVT`: electrical protection and liquid cooling exposure.
- `MOD`: data center cooling, but valuation and earnings event risk need attention.
- `COHR` and `CRDO`: optics/connectivity exposure, but expectations may already be high.
