# Review Cadence

## Weekly Review

Check for changes in:

- Hyperscaler capex headlines
- Data center construction and power availability
- AI chip and HBM supply commentary
- Major analyst estimate revisions
- New earnings dates
- Large price moves without thesis changes

## Earnings Season Review

For each company, search the transcript and release for:

- capex
- AI
- data center
- backlog
- orders
- book-to-bill
- lead time
- margin
- inventory
- customer concentration
- capacity expansion

Update:

- thesis
- signal
- downside trigger
- score
- next review date

## Exit Or Downgrade Triggers

Downgrade a watchlist name when:

- Backlog growth slows for two quarters
- Management lowers guidance
- Stock rerates while estimates do not move
- Gross margin peaks and starts falling
- Customer capex guidance weakens
- Inventory rises faster than revenue
- The company loses share to a competitor

## Upgrade Triggers

Upgrade a watchlist name when:

- Customers raise capex
- Backlog accelerates
- Orders exceed revenue growth
- Management raises guidance
- Analysts raise future EPS estimates
- Valuation remains reasonable after the revision
